## Executive Summary

The Enterprise Organization Chart documents the leadership structure, departmental hierarchy, and Information Technology Services (ITS) organization supporting the Northwind Technologies Enterprise IT Modernization Project. It provides a centralized view of reporting relationships, functional responsibilities, and operational ownership across the enterprise.

This organizational model establishes clear accountability for executive leadership, business operations, enterprise infrastructure, help desk services, and future technology initiatives. By defining responsibilities and reporting relationships, Northwind Technologies promotes efficient decision-making, standardized service delivery, and consistent enterprise governance.

The Information Technology Services department operates as a centralized organization responsible for designing, implementing, securing, documenting, and supporting the enterprise infrastructure. The organizational structure reflects modern IT operational practices and provides a scalable foundation for future expansion, including Microsoft 365, cloud services, automation, and Security Operations Center (SOC) capabilities.

---

## Purpose

This document establishes the standard organizational structure for Northwind Technologies by defining executive leadership, departmental hierarchy, reporting relationships, and Information Technology Services responsibilities. It serves as the authoritative reference for organizational governance, operational ownership, and enterprise accountability throughout the Enterprise IT Modernization Project.

---

## Organizational Principles

Northwind Technologies follows a centralized IT governance model.

Principles include:

• Centralized Infrastructure
• Departmental Accountability
• Least Privilege Administration
• Enterprise Standardization
• Documentation First
• Automation First
